Project Overview

The Problem

The rapid growth of e-commerce and same-day delivery services has created a strong demand for faster, more efficient, and environmentally friendly logistics solutions. Traditional ground-based delivery systems are often limited by traffic congestion, fuel costs, and infrastructure constraints.

Autonomous drone delivery systems provide a promising alternative, but introduce complex challenges related to route planning and decision-making. A drone delivery network may contain multiple delivery locations with varying distances and spatial distributions.

Choosing a single routing algorithm for all scenarios is inefficient, as different algorithms perform optimally under different network conditions. Small networks benefit from lightweight traversal algorithms, while larger or denser networks require more advanced shortest-path techniques.

Furthermore, many routing systems lack explainability, making it difficult to justify why a particular algorithm was selected. This reduces trust and adaptability in autonomous systems.

The primary challenge addressed by this project is to design an adaptive, intelligent, and explainable routing system capable of dynamically selecting the most suitable algorithm for drone delivery operations.

The Solution

This project introduces an AI-driven drone delivery system that models delivery locations as a weighted graph. The system evaluates multiple graph algorithms simultaneously and assigns each a competitive score based on network characteristics such as size, density, and spatial spread.

Instead of relying on a fixed algorithm, the system dynamically selects the most appropriate routing strategy. The selected route is visualized on an interactive map, and a drone animation simulates real-world delivery.

Key Features

  • Multi-Algorithm Evaluation: Compares 12 routing algorithms dynamically.
  • AI-Based Decision Making: Selects algorithms based on network characteristics.
  • Explainable Recommendations: Clearly explains why an algorithm is chosen.
  • Interactive Visualization: Displays routes and simulates drone movement.
  • Scalable Design: Can be extended with constraints like battery and weather.
